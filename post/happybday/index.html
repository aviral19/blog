Happy Birthday Pandey

July 19, 2017

Reading time ~4 minutes

What happens when you are afraid to post on your best friend’s facebook timeline, well you creepy fuck, you write it on your personal blog. Umm, how should I begin ? Let it be a question and answer roumd. Kind of like the rapid fire one.

1.) When did Pandey become your friend ?
Oh that is easy, well let us see, oh fuck you don’t remember do you.
Wait I do - It was way back in class 6th I guess. Pandey was in section A, and she became friend with Rythm, my then crush. Pandey wasn’t a direct friend, she became my friend through Rythm.
We used to tell Pandey about our tales of mischief, and our common friend Siddhant. So yeah, I would say, class 6th.

2.) When did you guys come close ?
Why the fuck are you interested in my personal life so much. I mean how does it matters to you. Boy you don’t ask the questions around here, I do
Okay, this would be class 8th, Rhythm had left the school.
But I had this attachment to Pandey, it was some pleasure she gave me, she still does.
She is undoubtedly cute, you always want to keep one cute friend just in case you’re single, dying alone at 40, you can marry her then.

3.) Why did you never propose Pandey, if you had a crush on her ? Okay so you will go all personal on me then, here you go. I obviously had a crush on Pandey, I mean don’t get to see such beauty around, I never could approach her initially due to Rhythm, well she was my first love. And to be honest, I wasn’t sort of those ‘rangbaaz’ kids out there. I was the one with oil on his head, pants quite up his waist, and more like a nerd you can say. I don’t deny being a nerd, and nor has it let me to a downfall, but it does loses you some opportunities. (gains you some as well.)

4.) When did you realize you can never ask her out now ? It can be called class 10th I suppose. I mean we were so close to be called a relationship by everyone else, but we knew we were ‘only friends’, and it was perfect, I could not even dare to lose such a beautiful relationship. And even I was ‘lite’ by then, Pandey was someone who understood me like no other, I was the Prefect of Su-ur house by then (I still wonder why don’t they call the houses by their color name - simple Red House).
Pandey did a lot of work for Su-ur house, and hence we came close, we came close like no other. And then I had a crush on Surabhi (again all that nerdiness stopped me), and Pandey knew about it, that strengthened our bond like no other.

5.) All that is cool, do you still love her ? Like do you want her as your girlfriend ? I would say No to this. I mean a guy would be more than lucky to have Pandey by his side, I just know her too well, to be in a relationship with her. A relationship is more like a puzzle you solve, layer by layer, by showering your love and gaining trust. Pandey already has my utmost trust and I have hers. Also, now I don’t sexually feel about Pandey.
But I have seen you looking at her breasts sometimes.. tsk. tsk.
Will you please shut the fuck up, it was long long ago, and I am a guy, every guy does this.
And well, she has a beauty, why wouldn’t someone not want to admire her. You just have a very vague and rude way of saying things.

6.) Where do you see all of this heading ? If I have to keep a friend like Pandey, I would keep her till eternity. You don’t get such friends in real life. Sometimes I do think if Pandey is just my hallicunation. Well when others confirm that hallicunation it becomes a reality, and Pandey is that amazing reality which happened to me.
She has been with me through all my ups and downs, she has given me space when I was in a relationship, she has been throughout with me when I broke up, she was there with me when I got into BITS.
And here she is with me, right in the messenger box, waiting for my reply, thinking I am working with some senior, and here I am, a fool, writing all this and making her wait It is me who could write books about her, entire memoirs, maybe a day will come.
